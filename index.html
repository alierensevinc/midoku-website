Midoku - Mini Sudoku

A Fun and Fast Brain Puzzle Game

Ready for a fresh take on a classic puzzle game? Midoku - Mini Sudoku offers a compact and engaging 6x6 grid perfect for all skill levels. Train your logic and number skills with hundreds of expertly crafted puzzles designed to give your brain the perfect workout, anytime, anywhere.

Why You'll Love Midoku:

  • Compact 6x6 Grids: Enjoy faster games on a smaller grid, perfect for short breaks or commutes.
  • Multiple Difficulty Levels: Whether you're a beginner or an expert, find the perfect challenge with Easy, Medium, and Hard modes.
  • Helpful In-Game Tools: Use Hints, make notes with Pencil Marks, and correct mistakes with the Undo button.
  • Offline Play: No Wi-Fi? No problem! Play your favorite puzzles completely offline.
  • Clean and Elegant Design: A simple, intuitive interface that makes solving puzzles a joy.
